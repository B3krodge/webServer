Welcome to the World of Fruits

Apple

Apples are nutritious and come in a variety of colors, including red, green, and yellow.

Banana

Bananas are rich in potassium and make for a great snack on the go!

Orange

Oranges are a great source of Vitamin C and are deliciously juicy.

Grapes

Grapes can be eaten fresh or dried to make raisins, and they come in red, green, and black varieties.

Watermelon

Watermelon is a hydrating fruit, perfect for hot summer days!
