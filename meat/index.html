Cow and Pig Meat

Cow Meat

Cow meat, also known as beef, is rich in protein, iron, and other essential nutrients. It’s widely consumed worldwide in various forms, from steaks to ground beef.

Pig Meat

Pig meat, or pork, is known for its versatility in cooking. It’s enjoyed in sausages, bacon, and various cuts of meat, and it’s an excellent source of protein and vitamins.
